Common Questions

When is the best time to work out?

The best time to work out is when you can consistently make it to the gym. Some people prefer morning workouts for energy throughout the day, while others find evening workouts help them de-stress.

How do I overcome gym anxiety?

Start with off-peak hours, bring a friend, or consider working with a trainer initially. Remember that everyone started somewhere, and most gym-goers are focused on their own workouts.

How long until I see results?

Results vary person to person, but generally, you might notice improved energy and mood within weeks, and physical changes within 8-12 weeks of consistent training and proper nutrition.

Should I eat before working out?

It's personal preference, but having a light meal 1-2 hours before working out can provide energy for your session. Experiment to find what works best for you.
