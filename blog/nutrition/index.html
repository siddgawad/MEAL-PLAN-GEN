Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I eat?

While individual needs vary, we typically recommend 3 main meals and 2-3 healthy snacks throughout the day to maintain stable energy levels.

Do I need to count calories?

Calorie counting can be helpful but isn't always necessary. We focus on teaching portion control and mindful eating practices.
